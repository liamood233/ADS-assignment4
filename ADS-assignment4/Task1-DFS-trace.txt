Task 1 – DFS from A

Graph neighbors (given):
A: C, B, D
B: A, C, E, G
C: A, B, D
D: C, A
E: G, F, B
F: G, E
G: F, B

DFS steps:

Start A. Mark visited.
Go to first neighbor C.
C -> go to B (A already visited).
B -> go to E (A, C visited).
E -> go to G.
G -> go to F.
F has no unvisited neighbors, back to G.
G no new neighbors, back to E.
E no new, back to B.
B: next neighbor after E is G (visited), back to C.
C: next after B is D (not visited yet) -> go to D.
D all neighbors visited, back to C, then back to A.
Done.

Order visited: A, C, B, E, G, F, D
